DUNGEONS OF DAGGORATH (1982, DynaMicro) - command crib
=======================================================

Type commands at the "?" prompt and press RETURN/ENTER.  Every word
may be abbreviated to any unambiguous prefix ("P R T" = PULL RIGHT
TORCH).  DELETE rubs out; the parser echoes as you type.

MOVEMENT
  MOVE              M         one cell forward
  MOVE BACK         M B       backstep (also LEFT/RIGHT/AROUND)
  TURN LEFT/RIGHT   T L, T R  quarter turn
  TURN AROUND       T A       half turn
  CLIMB UP/DOWN     C U, C D  ladders (holes in ceiling/floor)

HANDS AND OBJECTS
  PULL LEFT/RIGHT <obj>   P R T     take from the floor into a hand
  GET  LEFT/RIGHT <obj>   G L SW    take from your pack into a hand
  STOW LEFT/RIGHT         S L      hand into pack
  DROP LEFT/RIGHT         D R      hand onto the floor
  USE  LEFT/RIGHT         U R      use what the hand holds
                                   (a TORCH must be USEd to light it!)
  REVEAL LEFT/RIGHT       R R      identify a magic item
  INCANT <word>                    speak a ring's word

FIGHTING AND LOOKING
  ATTACK LEFT/RIGHT       A R      strike with that hand
  LOOK                    L        redraw the 3-D view
  EXAMINE                 E        show pack + hands inventory

SAVE / LOAD (one slot)
  ZSAVE <name>            ZS A     save the game
  ZLOAD <name>            ZL A     restore it

THE HEART is your only meter: it beats faster as you exert yourself
or take wounds.  If it flutters, REST - if it stops, so do you.
Objects: TORCH (pine/lunar/solar), SWORD (wooden/iron/elvish),
SHIELD (leather/bronze/mithril), FLASK, RING, SCROLL (the seer's
vision map).  Five levels down lies the Wizard.

Classic opening: P R T / U R / P L SW / E / L ... then explore.
